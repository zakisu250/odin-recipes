Lasagna

Description

This is my mom's special homemade lasagna recipe with a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filling.

Ingredients

  • A blend of small-curd cottage cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Tomato
  • Meat
  • Leafy green salad
  • Crusty garlic bread

Steps

  1. Prepare the sauce with tomato and meat
  2. Prepare the place to cook the lasagna with
  3. Put a layer of lasagna and the sauce at the top
  4. Put Parmesan cheese and a sprinkle pf small-curd cottage cheese
  5. Repeat steps 3-4 until the plate is full
  6. Cook under the appropriate temperature
  7. Serve when ready
